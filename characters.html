Ruby Rose

As a leader and youngest member of team RWBY, Ruby Rose leads her teammates during challenging times. As the spark of the group, she encourages her teammates to overcome the impossible. She also makes difficult decisions on the behalf of the group while considering everyone's different beliefs.


Weiss Schnee

Weiss Schnee was initially cold towards her teammates as she leaned on her status as a daughter of one of the largest companies. As she breaks away from the protective bubble of her family, she learns the harsh truth of reality and slowly opens to her teammates. She eventually uses her position to aid the people around her.


Blake Belladona

As a Faunus, young Blake was an active member of "White Fang", an organization that pushed for a peaceful relationship between the Faunus and humans. The Faunus people look similar to humans, and they possess animal traits. Due to the growing differences in beliefs, Blake leaves the group and found her trueself as she fights alongside her teammates.

Yang Xiao Long

Yang's fiery personality makes her a skilled fighter in close combat. Once she has set her mind, Yang is determined to see the light at the end of tunnel, and she doesn't take no for an answer. She despites seeing people getting mistreated by powerful authorities. Her straightforwardness brings the team closer as she motivates the members to share about themselves.
