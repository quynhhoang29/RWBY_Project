Main Players in RWBY


Monty Oum

After the release of Haloid, an animated video, Oum attracted attention from industry leaders in 2007. He then created RWBY, and the animated series was released under Rooster Teeth company in 2013.


Lindsay Jones

They are the an actor and director. They have been the voice actor of Ruby Rose in RWBY since 2013.They also made several appearances in the podcast named "Internet Box".


Kara Eberle

She is the voice actress of Weiss Schnee. She parted ways with Rooster Teeth in 2015. However, she will continue to be appear in the RWBY series as a voice actor.


Arryn Zech

She is the voice actress of Blake Belladona. In addition to her work in RWBY, she also does voice acting in Red vs Blue, an animated series. Although she has stopped working exclusively for Rooster Teeth in 2005, she still provides voice work for the company.


Barbara Dunkelman

She is the voice actress for Yang Xiao Long in RWBY. She also leads the creative and marketing intiatives in Rooster Teeth as the Marketing and Community Manager.
