A Summary of RWBY

RWBY is set in the world of Remnant where Grimm, a collective force of monsters, constantly bring danger and instill fear on Remnant's occupants. Huntsman and Huntresses hone their fighting abilties at academies and battle agaisnt the Grimm. They wish to bring order to chaos and peace to fear to the everyone in the world. Started from different paths, each member of team RWBY crossed paths at Beacon Academy where they would receive trainings to become formidable Huntresses. Each day, their goals to become Huntresses are challenged by the harsh truth of reality. Are they ready for the hardships ahead?
